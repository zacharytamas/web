Older Projects

This is a collection of past projects I was involved in to some degree, with quick blurbs about my involvement.

MyChipley

MyChipley was a personal project I built and rebuilt for many years starting when I was fifteen years old but never actually got it off the ground: this was due mainly to a lack of business acumen in my teenage years and an adequate amount of shyness.

As envisioned, MyChipley would have been an online news source for the smalltown I grew up in, which had nothing similar that was of quality. The local newspaper was owned by an enormous news chain and was very impersonal and of poor quality. I wanted to build a new kind of "socal" (social-local) news company which leveraged the latest technology in web publishing to do new things the local printed paper either could not or would not think of doing.

I performed almost all work on MyChipley myself. Design, front-end and back-end development, server management. Regardless of whether the website ever took off, the process of building and rebuilding while working on every level of the product was a very enlightening one. I may have reinvented the wheel several times but I learned a lot about wheels.

I still believe the vision I had could work for the community in my hometown but unfortunately would require more time and funding than I have to get it really moving. I still keep the domain in my back pocket. =)

UWF Life

UWF Life was an iOS application I developed while a student at the University of West Florida. It featured an campus map with walking directions to any building. If you didn't know what building you needed to go to, you could search by the department and find the building that way. Campus Emergency numbers were available with dialing shortcuts. A directory of dining facilities on-campus was included with their regular hours for reference, allowing the app to tell you your food options at any time of day. Developed while learning Objective-C, iOS, and Cocoa on my own out of curiosity.

NWFMarketplace

NWFMarketplace was an extension of the MyChipley idea. It was an attempt at simplifying the vision to a smaller piece that could possibly have better chance of traction. Again, business inexperience led to this project not going very far but a lot was learned.

Chipola College

I was briefly employed by the community college I attended during high school. I proposed designs and HTML/CSS that ultimately influenced the first major redesign of the school's website in years.

City of Marianna

While still in high school, a friend/colleague and I were able to use our professional network to land a contract rebuilding a local city's government portal website. Was one of my first design and development projects and also one of my first professional uses of Django.
