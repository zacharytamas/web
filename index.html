Internet Explorer (8) Array.sort Unreliable 29 Jan 2014

Fought this dumb bug at work today. In sorting functions given to Array.prototype.sort() in JavaScript, if you reassign argument variables' values in the function, IE8 may completely break on you, sometimes resulting in one or both of your comparison values becoming undefined randomly. Though it makes no sense JavaScript-wise, to fix this bug, you have to declare your own, separate variables for your values.
