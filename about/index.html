About

I am a curious twentysomething irrevocably obsessed with new technology and what can be done with it. I'm a modern-day Renaissance man with interests across both art and science alike. I appreciate the power of an idea and the paths it can travel down. While I generally consider myself a developer, I do find myself doing a lot of design work as well as constantly exploring new concepts and technologies.

I attended high school in my hometown of Chipley, Florida where I graduated High Honors. From there I went to nearby community college Chipola College where I got my start in the traditional college classes (though I had dual-enrolled during my Sophomore, Junior, and Senior years of high school). Following Chipola I spent a year at the University of West Florida studying Computer Science.
